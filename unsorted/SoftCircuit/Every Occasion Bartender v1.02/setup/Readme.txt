Overview
--------
Contains a library of 321 cocktail recipes in 12 categories--from Acapulco to Zombie. Convenient search feature locates specific drinks, or lists all those drinks with a particular ingredient. You can add your own categories and drinks and there is even a feature to enter the names of friends and business associates and a list of their favorite drinks. Print recipes to any Windows-compatible printer.
