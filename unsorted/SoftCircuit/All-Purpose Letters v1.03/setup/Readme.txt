Overview
--------
Includes 211 sample letters in 19 categories carefully designed to help get your message across in just about any situation. Categories include Job Letters, Government Action, Employment, Organization Activities, Credit, Payments, Collections, Complaints, Adjustments, Thank you, Sales Letters, Agreements and Authorizations, Congratulations, Invitations and many more. This program makes it easy to browse sample documents and to keep track of those you customize.
