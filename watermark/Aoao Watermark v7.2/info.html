From Photo Watermark Software:
Aoao Watermark is a professional watermark creation software. It helps you add image and text to any of your pictures, protect your pictures' copyright and make your images more special. It is not only a watermarking tool, but also an useful and simple photos processor. It can crop and resize image size that you need,batch rename,adjust image quality,and support many kinds of picture formats. Aoao Watermark supports batch watermarking photos, as well. For example, Aoao Watermark only takes 10 seconds to add your sign to three hundred of pictures in 800x600Pixels.
